What Bora Bora, French Polynesian has to offer

Bora Bora has many things to offer. You can feed their endangered turtles. You can even go snorkeling with the endangered turtles and several other aquatic creatures/animals in the torquise colored water. There are so many oppertunities to go on tours and explore the island. They have alot of dinner and lunch with an activity packages. You also have a breathe taking view of Mt. Otemanu. That is only some of what Bora Bora has to offer.
